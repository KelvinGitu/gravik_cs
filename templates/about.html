Talent Drive Program

At Gravik Climate Solutions, we believe in harnessing the power of innovative minds to tackle climate change. Our Talent Drive Program is designed to identify, support, and amplify the efforts of university students in Kenya who are passionate about developing sustainable solutions to reduce carbon emissions and combat climate change.


What We Offer

  • Funding and Resources: Access to essential resources and funding to bring your climate solution ideas to life.
  • Mentorship: Guidance from experts in the field to refine and implement your projects effectively.
  • Platform for Impact: Opportunities to showcase your solutions to a broader audience, including stakeholders and potential investors.

Who Can Apply

University students in Kenya who are currently enrolled and have a project idea or interest in climate change solutions.

Join us in our journey to create a greener, more sustainable future. Apply now to become a part of the Talent Drive Program and turn your innovative ideas into reality!
